Marcus' Trip to Japan

I went shopping to get some last-minute clothes so I could be more fashion-forward when in Korea and Japan. As it turned out, there was a big sale this weekend at several stores in Tanger Outlets which I didn't know about but was very convenient.

Also completely coincidently, there was an Asian Festival at Tanger Outlets. It felt like there was a larger Korean representation than any other specific Asian country. I guess this is like a preview for my trip in less than a week. The festival had a good atmosphere and I thoroughly enjoyed this unexpected excursion.

Here are some pictures of the food I got at the Asian Festival. I got some Shrimp Shu Mai, a Sugarcane and Passionfruit drink, Chicken Dumplings, Octopus Tentacle on a Stick, a Strawberry Matcha Mochi Donut, Pork Belly Dumplings, and Coal-Grilled Beef on a Stick.
